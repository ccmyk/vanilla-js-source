Now New Next Website<\/h2><\/div>

Now New Next is a digital platform for knowledge and innovation (with a strong divulgative character) that was born, after the pandemic, as an initiative of Fuego Camina Conmigo's Agency, to connect differently with its brands.<\/p>\n

They reached out to me to do the UX and support them in the interface and interaction design for the website, working alongside the talented creative team of the agency.
\nThe result was a colorful modular website, ready to adapt to future content and integrations, with little elements of surprise and playfulness for the user. <\/p>\n
