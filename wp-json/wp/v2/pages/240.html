Enthusiastic about graphic design, typography, and the dynamic areas of motion and web-based animations.<\/br>Specialized in translating brands into unique and immersive digital <\/br>user experiences.<\/h2><\/div><\/div><\/section>
<\/div><\/div>
<\/div>credits_<\/div>
<\/div>Monika Pufflerova<\/div><\/div><\/div>

My work covers wireframing, visual design, art direction, UI design, motion and interactive prototypes. This approach enables me to create thoughtful web experiences by focusing on the brand’s character and intent. Overall, I love to pay attention to detail and care for design execution and emphasizing collaboration with stakeholders throughout all project phases.<\/p>\n

I enjoy working with enthusiastic people so, within the last couple of years, I\u2019ve been working with multiple design teams, in person (Vasava and Toormix<\/strong>) and remotely (Experience.monks<\/strong>, formerly Jam3<\/strong>), but also I\u2019ve been fortunate to work and collaborate with some talented freelances, studios and agencies (Porto Rocha, R\u00e9plica Studio, Rebeka Arce Studios, Feeld.co, Alex Hunting Studio, Hanzo<\/strong> and more), crafting meaningful and remarkable digital experiences for international brands (such as Google, Meta, Adidas, Inditex and Vibia).<\/p>\n

My work and explorations have received various recognitions, allowing me to join the Awwwards Jury<\/strong> in 2020 to judge the best of the web and be nominated as Independent of the Year in 2024<\/strong> by Awwwards. Some projects have been also recognized with Honorable Mentions and SOTD on Awwwards<\/strong>, FOTD on FWA<\/strong>, WOTD on CSS Design Award<\/strong>, Gold and Silver Laus prize by ADGFAD<\/strong>, Shortlisted on ADCE Awards<\/strong>, Features on Behance<\/strong> and highlights on different platforms.<\/p>\n

I also love to participate in lectures and talks at design universities in Barcelona, such as Elisava University, where I\u2019ve been collaborating as an associate professor in some postgraduate courses. I have also produced an online course on Domestika called \u201cDesigning an Interactive Web with Figma\u201d<\/a>, and I regularly take part in conferences at design events and festivals as Awwwards Conference Design Leaders<\/a> and Friends of Decimal.<\/a><\/p>\n

For enquiries, collaboration requests or job opportunities, don\u2019t hesitate to get in touch<\/a><\/p>\n<\/div><\/div><\/div><\/section>
